FOR UNIQUE NUMBER IN THE ARRAY
you dont need to brute force by counting, simply use the XOR operator
eg. 1 + 3 - 1 = 3
rg. 4 + 5 - 4 = 5

note that => 1^3^1 = 3
          => 5^1^3^4^3^5^1 = 4

          the order doesnt matter in xor, what matters is the unique element only, since 0^a = a and a^a = 0
